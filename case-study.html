AI Social Insights

Interactive Forecast Dashboard Case Study

Overview

AI Social Insights is a web-based analytics dashboard that leverages LSTM and Prophet models to forecast social media metrics for multiple clients and platforms. Built using Python and Bokeh, it enables marketing teams and analysts to understand trends, detect anomalies, and optimize campaign performance with AI-driven insights.

Problem

Marketing teams struggle with understanding performance trends across platforms like Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, etc. Most dashboards only report past data — they don’t predict future outcomes or highlight insights. This makes it difficult to allocate budgets or adjust strategies proactively.

Solution

I developed a lightweight AI dashboard using:

  • LSTM – For deep learning-based time series forecasting
  • Prophet – For fast and interpretable forecasts
  • Bokeh – For building interactive visualizations
  • Anomaly Detection – To flag unusual spikes or drops
  • AI Insights – To automatically tell whether a metric is rising, dropping, or stable

Results

The dashboard offers:

  • 60-day forecasts with model switching
  • Client, platform, and metric filtering
  • RMSE score to evaluate forecast accuracy
  • Automatic anomaly highlighting
  • Real-time AI-powered metric trend interpretation
